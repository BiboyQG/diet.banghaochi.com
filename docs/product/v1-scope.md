# V1 Scope

V1 is manual logging only:

- Calories, carbs, protein, fat, and water
- Training/rest day override
- Daily and weekly summaries
- Body-weight quick log
- JSON export
- Cloudflare Access protection

Deferred:

- AI meal parsing
- Barcode scanner
- Food database search
- Meal photos
- Apple Health
- Widgets and Live Activities
